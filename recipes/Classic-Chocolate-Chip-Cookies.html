Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

Servings: 24 cookies | Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 10 mins | Total Time: 25 mins

A timeless favorite, Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies are the perfect blend of soft, chewy, and chocolatey goodness. Ideal for gatherings, a companion to your afternoon tea, or simply a treat to satisfy your sweet tooth, these cookies are loved by all ages. Follow this simple recipe to bake your own batch of perfection.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup packed light-brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 cups semisweet and/or milk chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). In a small bowl, whisk together the flour and baking soda; set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the butter with both sugars; beat on medium speed until light and fluffy. Reduce speed to low; add the salt, vanilla, and eggs. Beat until well mixed, about 1 minute.
  3. Add flour mixture; mix until just combined. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  4. Drop heaping tablespoon-size balls of dough about 2 inches apart on baking sheets lined with parchment paper.
  5. Bake until cookies are golden around the edges, but still soft in the center, about 8 to 10 minutes. Remove from oven, and let cool on baking sheet 1 to 2 minutes. Transfer to a wire rack, and let cool completely.
