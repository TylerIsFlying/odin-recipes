Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Servings: 4 | Prep Time: 15 mins | Cook Time: 30 mins | Total Time: 45 mins

This classic Chicken Alfredo is a creamy, flavorful, and easy-to-make dish perfect for a comforting meal. Featuring fettuccine pasta tossed in a homemade Alfredo sauce and topped with juicy, grilled chicken, it's a favorite that's sure to impress.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 pound fettuccine pasta
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then grill until golden and cooked through. Let it rest before slicing.
  2. Cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add garlic and saut√© until fragrant.
  4. Add heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and the sauce is smooth.
  5. Toss the cooked pasta in the Alfredo sauce until well coated.
  6. Plate the pasta, top with sliced chicken, garnish with parsley, and serve immediately.
